How do I reset my calorie goal?

Go to Settings > Profile in the app and update your information. Your calorie goal will be recalculated automatically.

Is my data synced to the cloud?

No! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't have access to your food logs or personal information.

Why isn't a barcode recognized?

We use Open Food Facts database. If a product isn't found, try the photo analysis feature or manual entry instead.

How accurate is the AI food analysis?

Our AI provides estimates based on visual analysis. For the most accurate tracking, we recommend verifying or adjusting the values when needed.
