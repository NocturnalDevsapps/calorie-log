üîí Our Privacy Commitment

Calorie Log is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your health data should remain private and under your control at all times.

üì± Data We Collect

Calorie Log is designed to minimize data collection. Here's what we collect and don't collect:

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Food logs and meal entries you create
  • Photos of meals (stored only on your device)
  • Personal health metrics (weight, height, age, activity level)
  • Daily calorie goals and progress data
  • Favorite meals and custom entries

Data we do NOT collect:

  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics tracking in the app

üåê Third-Party Services

Calorie Log uses the following third-party services:

  • Open Food Facts API: When you scan a barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts to retrieve nutritional information. No personal data is shared during this process.
  • AI Analysis: If you use photo analysis features, images may be processed to identify food items. This processing is done to provide nutrition estimates.

üóëÔ∏è Data Deletion & Account Deletion

At Calorie Log, we believe you should have full control over your data. Since we do not require user accounts and store all your data locally on your device, you can manage and delete your information at any time without needing to contact us.

How to Request Data Deletion:

You can delete your data using any of the following methods:

  • In-App Deletion: Navigate to Settings > Data Management within the Calorie Log app to delete specific entries or clear your entire history.
  • App Uninstallation: Simply uninstalling the Calorie Log app from your device will permanently delete all locally stored data, including food logs, photos, and health metrics.
  • Email Request: If you have any concerns or wish to confirm the deletion of any data that may have been processed during AI analysis, you can contact Nocturnal Devs at nocturnaldevs@gmail.com.

Types of Data Deleted:

  • All food logs and meal history
  • Photos of meals stored within the app
  • Personal health metrics (weight, height, age, etc.)
  • Calorie goals and progress tracking data
  • Custom food entries and favorites

Data Retention & Exceptions:

  • Local Data: Once deleted via the app or uninstallation, this data is gone forever and cannot be recovered by Nocturnal Devs.
  • Processed Data: Images processed for AI analysis are used transiently and are not linked to any personal identifier. We do not retain these images on our servers after the analysis is complete.
  • Support Communications: If you contact us via email, we may retain that correspondence for support purposes for up to 24 months, unless you request its earlier deletion.
